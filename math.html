Talks

  • An Introduction to Descriptive Set Theory (University of Toronto Scarborough CMS Undergraduate Seminar, 2023): A crash course on Polish spaces, followed by an introduction to the notions of measure and category, with a discussion of how classical theorems on Polish spaces can be used to prove the Erdős-Sierpiński duality between measure and category. The recording is available here.
  • Basic Embedding Results in Descriptive Set Theory (University of Toronto Mississauga, 2023): A brief introduction to Polish spaces, followed by a discussion of classical embedding results involving the the Hilbert Cube, the Cantor space, and the Baire space.
